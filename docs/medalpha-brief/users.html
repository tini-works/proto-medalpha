Users

Who we serve and how they use MedAlpha

Personas

Five key user segments spanning digital literacy levels from very high to moderate.

S
Sarah
34, Working Mother
Goal
Manage family health efficiently without sacrificing time
Key Needs
  • Quick booking for whole family
  • Prescription refills for children
  • Telemedicine for minor ailments
Quits if: Too slow, can't book for family members easily
Digital Skill: High
M
Marc
42, Health-Conscious Professional
Goal
Proactive health management with maximum convenience
Key Needs
  • On-demand consultations
  • Preventive check-ups
  • Time efficiency above all
Quits if: Wastes his time, poor video quality
Digital Skill: Very High
H
Helga
68, Senior with Chronic Conditions
Goal
Reliably manage medications and reduce travel burden
Key Needs
  • Simple prescription flow
  • Accessible, large UI
  • Step-by-step guidance
Quits if: Confusing flow, too many steps, unclear outcomes
Digital Skill: Moderate
E
Elena
23, Wellness Enthusiast
Goal
Affordable, trendy health and beauty solutions
Key Needs
  • Discreet ordering
  • Dermatology consults
  • Modern, fast experience
Quits if: Feels outdated, not discreet enough
Digital Skill: Very High
T
Thomas
51, Value-Driven Pragmatist
Goal
Save money and time on routine health needs
Key Needs
  • Transparent pricing
  • Clear confirmations
  • No hidden complexity
Quits if: Hidden costs, unclear confirmations
Digital Skill: Moderate
