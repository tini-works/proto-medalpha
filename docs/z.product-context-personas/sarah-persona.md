- **Occupation:** Marketing Manager (Part-Time)

- **Digital Proficiency:** High

## 2. Goals, Motivations, and Jobs-to-Be-Done
- **Primary Goal:** Manage family's health and household needs efficiently without sacrificing quality time
- **Motivations:** 
  - Time-saving and convenience; values efficiency over price
  - Peace of mind knowing family health is managed in one place
  - Loyalty rewards (Payback points) for family purchases
  - Features that reduce decision-making burden
  - Feel like a 'good parent' managing health proactively
- **Jobs-to-Be-Done:**
  - Handle all family's health and drugstore needs while on the go
  - Easy upload of e-prescriptions for children's recurring medications
  - Quick video consultation for minor ailments to avoid time off work
  - Seamless scheduling of in-store health checks during shopping trips
- **Quote:** "I need a single app that lets me handle all my family's health and drugstore needs while I'm on the go."

  - Actively uses Click & Collect for convenience; rarely visits store without app
  - Recently enabled push notifications for medication reminders
  - Frequently leaves app reviews praising family-friendly features
- **Behavioral Patterns:**
  - Opens app 3-4 times per week, primarily during lunch breaks and evenings
  - Spends avg. 8-10 minutes per session browsing deals and managing prescriptions
  - Prefers iPhone; syncs app with family calendar for appointment reminders
  - Abandons app if loading time exceeds 3 seconds
  - Shares positive experiences on WhatsApp family group chat
- **Language/Culture Context:**
  - Native German speaker; prefers German language interface
  - Values clear, direct communication without jargon
  - Responds well to family-oriented messaging and inclusive imagery
  - Expects professional, trustworthy tone reflecting healthcare context
  - Appreciates humor and relatable scenarios in app copy

- **Unmet Needs:**
