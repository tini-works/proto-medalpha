# Helga Persona

## 1. User Types and Personas
- **Name:** Helga
- **Age:** 68
- **Occupation:** Retired Teacher
- **Location:** Small town, relies on public transport
- **Digital Proficiency:** Moderate
- **Health Status:** Manages Type 2 diabetes and hypertension
- **Color:** #0066cc

## 2. Goals, Motivations, and Jobs-to-Be-Done
- **Primary Goal:** Reliably manage long-term medication and reduce physical burden of travel for routine care
- **Motivations:**
  - Health security and peace of mind; fears missing medications
  - Reducing physical burden and travel-related stress
  - Reliability and consistency; prefers predictable app behavior
  - Simplicity and clarity; avoids unnecessary complexity
  - Maintain independence while accepting help when needed
- **Jobs-to-Be-Done:**
  - Get medication to arrive on time and speak to a doctor without leaving the house
  - Simple, step-by-step process for uploading recurring e-prescriptions
  - Routine follow-up appointments for chronic conditions from home
  - Easy-to-use interface for booking in-store assistance or health services
- **Quote:** "I just want my medication to arrive on time and to speak to a doctor without having to leave the house."

## 3. Behaviors, Habits, and Context of Use
- **Typical Day:**
  - 8:00 AM: Morning routine; takes daily diabetes and blood pressure medications
  - 10:00 AM: Calls daughter to ask for help uploading e-prescription via dm app
  - 2:00 PM: Rests; watches TV; checks app for medication delivery status
  - 4:00 PM: Telemedicine appointment with doctor for routine diabetes follow-up
  - 6:00 PM: Evening routine; sets app reminder for next day's medication
- **App History:**
  - Reluctant adopter; daughter convinced her to download in 2024
  - Initially struggled with app navigation; now relies on simplified interface
  - Prefers phone calls to customer support; appreciates human assistance
  - Uses app primarily for prescription management, not browsing or shopping
  - Grateful for delivery service; reduces need to travel in poor weather
- **Behavioral Patterns:**
  - Opens app 1-2 times per week, primarily for prescription and appointment management
  - Spends avg. 3-5 minutes per session; prefers straightforward, linear workflows
  - Uses older Android smartphone; needs large text and high contrast
  - Relies on written instructions or video tutorials for complex tasks
  - Calls family members for technical support rather than exploring app independently
- **Language/Culture Context:**
  - Native German speaker; prefers simple, clear German without technical jargon
  - Responds well to reassuring, supportive tone reflecting care and concern
  - Appreciates large fonts, high contrast, and simple visual design
  - Values step-by-step guidance and confirmation messages
  - Prefers phone support and human interaction over automated systems

- **Pain Points:**
  - Complexity of the German e-prescription system and how to use it
  - Difficulty traveling to doctor's office or pharmacy, especially in bad weather
  - Worry about missing medication refills or appointments
  - Limited confidence with complex digital interfaces
- **Unmet Needs:**
  - Simplified e-prescription uploading process
  - Reliable home delivery to avoid travel
  - Easy telemedicine for chronic condition follow-ups
  - Step-by-step guidance and human support for app use
