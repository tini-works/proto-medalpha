Stay healthy this winter
Tips for keeping your immune system strong.
Get Enough Sleep
Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night. Sleep is when your body repairs and regenerates.
Stay Active
Regular moderate exercise can boost your immune function. Even a 30-minute daily walk helps.
Eat Well
Focus on fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Vitamin C and D are especially useful in winter.
Stay Hydrated
Drink plenty of water and warm beverages to keep your body functioning well.
