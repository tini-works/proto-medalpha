Appointments
How do I book an appointment?
Navigate to the Appointments tab and tap "Book Appointment". Select your doctor, date, and time slot.
Can I reschedule or cancel?
You can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before the scheduled time.
How do telemedicine consultations work?
Telemedicine consultations are conducted via secure video call. Ensure a stable connection.
Prescriptions
How do I redeem a prescription?
Go to Prescriptions > Redeem and choose home delivery or pharmacy pickup.
What is NFC prescription scanning?
NFC allows you to securely scan your electronic health card to redeem prescriptions.
Account & Privacy
How is my health data protected?
Your data is protected with encryption and complies with GDPR and German regulations.
Can I delete my account?
Yes. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Data will be removed within 30 days.
